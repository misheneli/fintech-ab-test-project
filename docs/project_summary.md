# Project Summary

## Objective
Determine if a 30-day free trial for the premium subscription increases user LTV compared to the immediate paywall.

## Results
- Conversion rate increased from 2% (control) to 5% (treatment).
- However, LTV remained statistically unchanged due to higher churn after the trial.
- Users who made a deposit during the trial showed 3x higher LTV.

## Conclusion
Do not roll out the free trial broadly. Instead, consider targeting users who have made an initial deposit.
